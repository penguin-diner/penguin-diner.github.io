Welcome to Penguin Diner

Join Penny the Penguin on an exciting culinary adventure in Penguin Diner - the ultimate time management game where you take on the role of a restaurant owner and help Penny turn her diner into the hottest spot in town!

Run Your Own Penguin Restaurant

In Penguin Diner, you'll step into Penny's shoes and manage every aspect of her bustling restaurant. From seating customers and taking orders to cooking delicious meals and serving them with a smile, it's your job to ensure that every guest leaves happy and satisfied.

Serve Delicious Dishes to Hungry Customers

With a mouthwatering menu featuring a variety of dishes, including fish and chips, burgers, salads, and more, there's something for everyone at Penguin Diner. Use your time management skills to juggle multiple orders at once and deliver each meal with speed and precision.

Upgrade Your Restaurant and Expand Your Business

As you progress in Penguin Diner, you'll earn coins that you can use to upgrade your restaurant and unlock new features. Expand your seating area, add new items to your menu, and hire additional staff to help you keep up with the demand.

Experience Penguin Diner on the Go

Whether you're at home or on the go, Penguin Diner is the perfect way to pass the time and indulge your passion for cooking and time management games. Play on your desktop, laptop, or mobile device and immerse yourself in Penny's world of penguin hospitality!
